Steve Jobs

1955-2011

Father of the Digital Revolution


He was an American entrepreneur ,marketer and inventor, was the co-founder (along with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne), chairman as well as CEO of Apple Inc. Through Apple ,Steve Jobs was also served as chief executive of Pixar Animation Studios; he became a member of the BODs of The Walt Disney Company in 2006, when Disney acquired Pixar. He also lead a great role in launching the Laser Writer, one of the first widely available laser printers into the market.


Jobs left Apple in 1985 as there were some misunderstandings with the BODs and founded NeXT, a computer platform development company which worked in the field of higher-education and business markets. In 1996, after the failure to deliver its operating system, Copland, Gil Amelio; Apple turned to NeXT Computer, and the NeXTSTEP platform became the foundation for the Mac OS X. Jobs returned to Apple as an advisor, and took control of the company as an interim CEO. Jobs brought Apple from near bankruptcy to profitability by 1998.


After getting his previous designation as CEO of the company, Jobs looked forward in the development of many new gadgets and software's and began to work in the field of technology.He transformed his career in the computer and consumer electronics fields, transforming "one industry after another”.


Steve Jobs received a number of honors and public recognition for his influence in the technology and music industries. He is also called a "futurist and “new technology inventor”.


He has been described as the "Father of the Digital Revolution", "master of innovation".I have very much influenced with him.
